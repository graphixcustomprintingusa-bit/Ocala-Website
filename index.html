About Our Company

Graphix Custom Apparel operates as a local custom print shop located in Ocala, Florida. We take great pride in our work, ensuring that our clients always leave satisfied and with smiles on their faces. Known for our swift turnaround times, we prioritize both quality and service, setting the standard in Central Florida.

Our team is committed to bringing your creative ideas to life on apparel of all kinds. From printing and embroidery to custom vinyl graphics, we handle every project with meticulous attention to detail. We believe that custom apparel is more than just clothing—it's a statement of identity and a way to build community, and we're here to help you make yours.

Our Services

We specialize in a variety of custom apparel and promotional items to bring your ideas to life.

Printing

High-quality custom apparel printing using a variety of styles, including DTF (Direct to Film) and traditional Screenprinting, for a wide range of products like t-shirts, hoodies, and more.

Embroidery

Professional embroidery services to add a premium, textured look to your hats, polos, jackets, and other garments.

Promotional Items

Custom branded merchandise like mugs, pens, and keychains, as well as business cards, banners, yard signs, and car magnets, to help you promote your business or event.
